    We specialize in providing top-notch rebar detailing services tailored specifically for the North America market. With our industry expertise, cutting-edge technology, and commitment to excellence, we deliver accurate and comprehensive rebar detailing solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and precision.

    With years of experience in the rebar detailing industry, our team of skilled and knowledgeable professionals understand the unique requirements and regulations of the North America market.

    We have successfully completed numerous projects, ranging from small-scale residential buildings to large-scale commercial and infrastructure developments.

    We harness the power of the latest software and advanced technology to provide precise and efficient rebar detailing services. Our team is proficient in using industry-leading software such as Tekla Structures, ensuring accurate 3D modeling, clash detection, and visualization of the reinforcement details.

    Compliance with local codes and standards is of utmost importance in the North America market. Our team is well-versed in the specific regulations and requirements set forth by authorities such as the American Concrete Institute (ACI) and the American Institute of Steel Construction (AISC). We ensure that all our rebar detailing work adheres to these guidelines.

    We understand the significance of effective communication and collaboration in project success. Our team works closely with architects, engineers, contractors, and other stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle, ensuring that their expectations are met, and all design and construction elements are accurately incorporated.

    Our Services:

    Our skilled team of detailers creates detailed reinforcement drawings, including plans, sections, and schedules, as per your project requirements. We ensure that all elements such as rebar size, shape, spacing, and lap lengths are precisely documented, facilitating smooth implementation during construction.

    We generate comprehensive bar bending schedules that provide contractors with the exact quantities and specifications of reinforcing steel required for each element of the structure. This helps streamline the procurement and installation process, reducing material waste and ensuring project efficiency.

    Using advanced software, we develop detailed 3D models of the reinforcement, allowing for better visualization and coordination of design elements. We perform clash detection to identify any conflicts between rebar and other building components, mitigating potential issues and minimizing rework.

    We provide accurate as-built drawings, capturing any modifications or changes made during the construction phase. These drawings serve as vital documentation for future reference, maintenance, and renovations.

    We are dedicated to delivering exceptional rebar detailing services that meet and exceed client expectations. Our commitment to precision, efficiency, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the North America market.
