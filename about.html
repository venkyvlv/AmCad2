    AmCAD Solutions is a Bangalore, India based Engineering services company providing design and detailing support to North American construction and infrastructure industry.

    We specialize in design and detailing of Structural Steel, PEMB/EMB, Tilt-up Panel, Rebar and miscellaneous Detailing and Industrial Piping support. We are working with General contractors, PEB fabricators, Steel Fabricators, concrete and tilt-up contractors and EPC companies across USA and Canada.

    We primarily started as steel detailing company and today we have extended our services to various industries that include but not limited to Construction, Oil & Gas, Plant Engineering and Mechanical Industry.

    Our in-house team of professionals include Engineers, Modelers, checkers and drafters, catering to clients across North America. We are cognizant with AISC, NISD, CISC, TCA, ACI ASTM, ASME and ACI codes.

    We have a dedicated Project Manager for each team who will be your single point of contact and take care of schedule, performance and quality. Our execution process is driven by inhouse developed SOPs and checklists; we are ISO 9001:2015 certified for Quality process by “Quality Management System” accredited by UASL. We are active members of AISC and NISC trade association.

    Be it large commercial building, industrial structure or small miscellaneous detailing jobs, we could help you!!

    We start by understanding your detailing standards and fabrication methods to make sure you get drawings with ease to manufacture in a format that you would prefer. Our Project Managers will be your single point of contact to answer all your concerns throughout the project phase.

    Our team is trained to provide clean, accurate and comprehensive drawings on schedule. We are committed to our client’s success by working diligently to meet customer’s demands and provide the highest quality of deliverables.
